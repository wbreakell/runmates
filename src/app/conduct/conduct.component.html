Code of Conduct

These community guidelines represent the rules, norms, and responsibilities we expect you to uphold as an upstanding member of the RunMates community.

Be nice

Be kind to others and avoid offensive language or negative comments. We know harassment when we see it and moderators are empowered to suspend your account.

Stick to the topic

This site is for runners looking for running experiences. Please keep it to that.

Be honest

Experiences are best when expectations are met or exceeded. If you're a host, offer some details about the run. If you're a traveler, be gracious with your host. In all cases, be up front about your fitness level.

Give back

If you're typically a traveler, consider hosting an occasional run at your home base.

Help us improve

Share your feedback about your experiences and suggestions for things you'd like to see and ways we can get better.
